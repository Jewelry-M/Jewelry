Our Story

A Legacy of Flavor & Tradition

Founded in 1987 by the Rossi family, Trattoria Aurelia brings the soul of Italian countryside dining to the modern table. Every dish is a tribute to generations of nonnas, farmers, and artisans who believed that food is love made visible.

We source our ingredients daily from trusted producers across Italy — from San Marzano tomatoes to Piedmont truffles — ensuring every plate tells a story of authenticity and passion.
